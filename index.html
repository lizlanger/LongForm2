Course Goals


1. Consider the role of rhetoric, technology, and ethics in acts of making.

Throughout the course of this class, I have been able to incorporate the role of rhetoric, technology, and ethics in all of my projects thus far. Specifically, incorparating these skills into my Good Design project, where we delt with writing in design context and using digital media to create our final product. I have learned to rely on rhetoric to guide the course content, using technology to make the projects, and ethics in terms of putting my best work forward. This class has allowed me to use these skills and gain more.

2. Leverage Design Principles.

Using the Principles of Design, I have been able to create well balanced and structured pieces of work. I relied on these principles most heavily in my Logo Design project. I had to think about what would best appeal to my audience as well as what looked good in terms of different designs. I now know that there are rules to good design, as well as the need for a general liking of the work from the artist themselves.

3. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

I have been able to work with others on in-class assignments, gaining a well rounded knowledge of my peers as well as their work. The workshops have allowed me to undertsand the different mediums and different designs of this course. The most beneficial peer workshop was the Logo Design workshop. Here I was able to expand more on my interviewee's perspectives while gaining live peer responses as well. It was intresting to be able to compare the two.



4. Practice revision within and across media forms.

Receiving feedback and revising our work has been a large part of this course. It is beneficial to understand where there is room for improvement and the chance to redo projects to create the best version possible. I especially learned the value of iterating on my designs when redesigning my Good Design project, as I had to adjust my text boxes and even the text within to best fit the context of the project.

5. Learn to present your work.

Throughout this course, I have been able to present my work in front of class and to gain valuable, live feedback. It has helped essentially in my public speaking skills and my ability to take criticism from a live audiance. I feel as if I did best in presenting when sharing my Logo Design project. Using the smaller, study group made me more confident in recieveing feedback and learning how to ask engaging questions.
